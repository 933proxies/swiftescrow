How Escrow Works

  1. Agreement: Parties agree terms and initiate escrow.
  2. Deposit: Buyer deposits BTC to escrow address.
  3. Delivery: Seller delivers goods/services.
  4. Confirm & Release: Buyer confirms, funds released to seller.
  5. Dispute: If required, manual review by support.
